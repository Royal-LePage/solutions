# Spring Cleaning & Decluttering

It’s that time ofyear again! Springtime brings sunshine, rejuvenation, and excitement for thesummer days ahead. 

Spring is a greattime to do some deep-cleaning and decluttering, especially if you’re moving.Packing your belongings is the perfect chance to get rid of those old items youno longer need.

## DeclutteringTips:

When deciding whatto keep, a good rule of thumb is that every item should fall into one of thesecategories:

1.     CurrentlyUseful

·        Theseare everyday things you use. Other than the obvious items, this could also beitems you use once a week or month. 

2.     Couldbe Useful

·        Maybeduring your packing and organizing, you found an item you forgot about. Nowthat you’ve found it, it’s a good idea to move it to a spot in your house whereyou will see it and remember to use it more. 

3.     Nostalgic

·        Humansare very nostalgic, and we like to keep certain items because of the memoriesor history they have. And that’s okay!

If the item doesnot fall into these categories easily, or you find yourself justifying how itcould possibly fit into these categories, then it’s time to discard, donate, orrecycle it.

Battling theWhat If’s:

Sometimes it canbe challenging to get rid of things because we think “what if I need that.” Chancesare, if you haven’t needed it in the last year or two, you probably won’t. Andif a situation comes up in the future where you need an item like it, you canbuy a new one, which is usually safer and more sanitary if the current one istoo old/out-of-date. 

DonationTips: 

For anyclothes/shoes donations, please ensure the items are of good quality (nostains, sweat marks, rips/tears.) If it’s something you wouldn’t wear anymoredue to unpleasant qualities, best to discard it instead. 

## Cleaning Tips:

Start with a planand work through one room at a time! 

During the yearit’s normal to keep up with regular household tasks such as cleaning thefloor/carpet, countertops, dishes, and laundry. Spring cleaning is the time toadd deep-cleaning tasks such as:

·        TheWindows

·        DoorTracks/Frames

·        Baseboards

·        UnderAppliances & Furniture

·        Insidethe Fridge (throw away expired items)

·        Insidethe Oven & Microwave

·        Hoseout the trash bin

·        DustLight Fixtures, House Plants, & Blinds

·        Gutters(ensure drainage is clear)

·        GarageFloor/Driveway

Take your cleaningand decluttering one step at a time and try to enjoy the process. Or maybesimply enjoy the result of a fresh home!
