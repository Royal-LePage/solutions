Key Brand Rules

Always Use "Royal LePage" Together

The word "Royal" must never appear alone. It must always be used in conjunction with "LePage".

Trademark Symbols Required

Use ® or ™ in the most prominent position (headline, cover). In presentations, place on every page or slide.

No Blue, Yellow, or Gold

These colours must never be used in connection with Royal LePage trademarks under any circumstances.

Minimum Logo Sizes

Master brand logo: 0.75" minimum. Brokerage logo: 1.5" minimum. Never scale below these thresholds.

Staging Area (Clear Space)

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the uppercase letters in "ROYAL LePAGE" on all sides of the logo.

Do Not Alter the Logo

Never place the logo in a box, add drop shadows, change colours, scale disproportionately, or remove the trademark symbol.

Include Disclaimer Text

All materials must include: "Royal LePage® Solutions is independently owned and operated." with a reference to rlp.ca/notices.

Broker/Manager Approval Required

All marketing and advertising materials must meet your broker's/manager's approval before publishing. Provincial guidelines take precedence.
