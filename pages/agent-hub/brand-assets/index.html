Key Brand Rules

Always Use "Royal LePage" Together

The word "Royal" must never appear alone — always in conjunction with "LePage".

Trademark Symbols Required

Use ® or ™ in the most prominent position. In presentations, place on every page or slide.

No Blue, Yellow, or Gold

These colours must never be used in connection with Royal LePage trademarks under any circumstances.

Minimum Logo Sizes

Master brand logo: 0.75" minimum. Brokerage logo: 1.5" minimum. Never scale below these sizes.

Staging Area (Clear Space)

Maintain clear space equal to the height of "ROYAL LePAGE" uppercase letters on all sides of the logo.

Do Not Alter the Logo

Never add drop shadows, change colours, place in a box, scale disproportionately, or remove the ® symbol.

Include Disclaimer Text

All materials must state "independently owned and operated" and reference rlp.ca/notices (except business cards and signage).

Broker/Manager Approval Required

All marketing materials must meet your broker's/manager's approval. Provincial guidelines take precedence.
