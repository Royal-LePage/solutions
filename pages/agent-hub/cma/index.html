Module 10 · Strategy
Offer Strategy
Coordinated launch & offer holdback Pro strategy
List Thursday. Showings Friday–Monday. Offers Tuesday evening. Controlled urgency — buyers know others are looking, driving competing offers and eroding conditions.
Multiple offer presentation If we get there
Accept best, counter one, or call for highest and best. I present every offer in full and advise on strategy — you always make the final call. Goal: highest price and cleanest deal.
Bully offers Know this in advance
Arrives before offer date to pressure immediate acceptance. Alberta law requires me to present all offers. Advice: counter at a price that makes the disruption worth it — or ignore and hold the plan.
Subject-free vs. subject-to
A clean offer is preferred but don't dismiss a financed offer with strong pre-approval and short conditions. I'll vet every buyer's financing before advising which offer to choose.
Price reductions — if needed
No offer in 10 days — we revisit the data together. Updated DOM trends, showing feedback, new comps. A targeted reduction is strategic, not a failure. We plan for this before signing.
