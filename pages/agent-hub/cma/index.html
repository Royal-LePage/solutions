Multiple offer presentation If we get there
Accept best, counter one, or call for highest and best. I present every offer in full — you always make the final call. Goal: highest price and cleanest deal.
Bully offers Know this in advance
Arrives before offer date to pressure immediate acceptance. Alberta law requires presentation. Advice: counter at a price that makes the disruption worth it — or hold the plan.
Subject-free vs. subject-to
