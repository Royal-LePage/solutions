Royal LePage Shelter Foundation

Giving back to our community is an important part of who we are.

A home should be a safe place — and helping people feel safe, supported, and at home is at the heart of what we do.

Through the Royal LePage Shelter Foundation, we support local women's shelters and programs that help women and children experiencing domestic violence. The Foundation is Canada's largest public foundation dedicated solely to supporting women's shelters and violence prevention programs.

Since it began in 1998, the Royal LePage Shelter Foundation has helped raise millions of dollars for shelters across Canada. One thing that makes the Foundation special is that Royal LePage Canada covers the administrative costs — which means every dollar raised by Royal LePage agents and brokerages goes directly to the cause.

Why This Matters

A Safe Place to Call Home

Sadly, many women and children in Canada are forced to leave their homes because of domestic violence. Women's shelters provide a safe place to stay, along with support, guidance, and resources during an incredibly difficult time.

By supporting the Royal LePage Shelter Foundation, we're helping local shelters continue this important work — and helping create safer homes and stronger communities.

"Real estate is about more than buying and selling houses. It's also about helping people feel safe, supported, and at home."

How Royal LePage Solutions Helps

Commission Donors

Our agents can choose to become commission donors, giving a portion of their real estate commissions to the Shelter Foundation. This keeps support going all year — not just during one-time events.

We support local fundraising efforts and community initiatives that raise both awareness and funds for shelters right here in our area.

Community Events

From local events to shelter-focused campaigns, our team takes part in efforts throughout the year that strengthen support for families in our communities.
